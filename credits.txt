==============================
CANVAS FILTER ALGORITHMS
==============================

1. Greyscale (ITU-R BT.601 Luminance)
   Formula: Y = 0.299R + 0.587G + 0.114B
   Source: International Telecommunication Union
   Reference: CCIR Report 601 (now ITU-R BT.601)

2. Sepia Tone Filter
   RGB transformation matrix from color science literature
   Standard vintage photograph effect

3. Invert Filter
   Simple RGB color inversion: R' = 255 - R
